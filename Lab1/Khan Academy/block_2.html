The beach stretches endlessly, a serene blend of soft, golden sand and the rhythmic whispers of the sea. Tiny grains glisten under the sun, warm beneath bare feet, while the salty breeze carries the scent of the ocean and the distant cry of seagulls. Gentle waves roll ashore, leaving delicate patterns of foam before retreating, their cadence soothing and timeless. Scattered seashells and smooth pebbles dot the shoreline, treasures shaped by the water’s patient touch. Beyond the sand, tufts of dune grass sway lazily, their green blades contrasting against the bright, cloud-dappled sky. The horizon melts into shades of blue where sea and sky meet, an endless expanse that invites both quiet reflection and a sense of wonder at nature’s vast simplicity.
